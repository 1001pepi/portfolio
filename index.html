Ramses Tanankem was born in Bafoussam, Cameroun. In 2023, he graduated from Polytechnique Yaoundé and obtained his Computer Science Engineering degree. He completed his end-of-study internship in the WLResearch research laboratory during which he worked on building a formalism and designing experiments to model codon usage bias and build better Machine Learning models for codon optimization. This internship will be his first contact with the world of research.

In 2023, after his Computer Science Engineering degree, he was admitted at ENSIMAG in Grenoble, France to follow a Master 2 program in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence. Since February 2024, he is a Data Engineer intern at Neoxia in Grenoble.
